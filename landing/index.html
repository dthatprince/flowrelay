The Problem We Solve

Why Hozhopath?

Children in protective care frequently require transportation between foster placements, family visitations, court appointments, schools, and medical facilities. Without centralized monitoring systems, agencies often rely on manual coordination and fragmented documentation.

Flow Relayeliminates these challenges by providing a secure digital platform that tracks, verifies, and documents every transportation event from start to finish.

The result is improved safety, better accountability, and stronger compliance with child welfare regulations.

Platform Capabilities

Key Features

Real-Time Transportation Monitoring

Track transportation routes live with secure GPS monitoring. Supervisors and caseworkers gain visibility into the location and status of every child transport event.

Secure Transport Scheduling

Caseworkers can easily schedule pickups and drop-offs while coordinating transportation providers through a centralized system.

Chain-of-Custody Documentation

Every transfer is logged with timestamps, GPS verification, and digital signatures to ensure a secure and verifiable custody trail.

Driver and Vehicle Verification

Ensure only authorized and verified drivers transport children with identity authentication and credential verification.

Incident Alerts and Notifications

Receive automatic alerts for delays, route deviations, or emergency situations to ensure quick response and intervention.

Compliance and Audit Reporting

Generate transportation logs and compliance reports suitable for internal audits, regulatory reviews, and court documentation.
